Catholic Prayers
Through the centuries the Catholic Church has encouraged Christians to pray in Spirit and in Truth[1]. So that Catholic Faithful may be able to grow spiritually the Church has recorded various prayers and promoted devotional practices.
     The Church has formulated prayers to help Christians draw closer to God and find strength and courage in Him. Many of these prayers are rooted in Holy Scripture - the Holy Bible - while others have been revealed to Christian Saints through mystical experiences, visions and apparitions.
     These formulaic prayers have brought immense graces to Christians around the world, resulting in miracles in the lives of faithful Christians, and explosions of faith in places where the Church is persecuted.
     We hope this repository of prayers helps you in your own journey towards Eternal Life in Jesus Christ.
